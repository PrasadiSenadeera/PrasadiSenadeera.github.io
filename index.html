Academic Qualifications

Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies (Erasmus Mundus)

This program is being carried out in collaboration with Information management school, New University of Lisbon (NOVA IMS) in Portugal, the Institute for Geoinformatics (IFGI), University of Münster (WWU) in Germany and the Universitat Jaume I (UJI) in Spain.

Main Subjects: Geostatistics, Remote Sensing, GIS, Geospatial data mining, Reference Systems

Bachelors of Surveying Science (Specialization: Photogrammetry and Remote sensing)

Awarded by the Faculty of Geomatics, Sabaragamuwa university of Sri Lanka, Balangoda (Sri Lanka)

Main Subjects: Remote Sensing, Geographic Information Systems, Geodesy, Photogrammetry, Land Surveying, Hydrographic Surveying, Cartography, Computer Programming.

Organized by Faculty of Marine Technology, Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology (TUMSAT)

Main Subjects: Introduction to QZSS, GLONASS, BeiDou and Galileo Signals, Navigation message structures of GNSS signals, Front- end Architectures, Differential GPS Real-time positioning using GNSS
